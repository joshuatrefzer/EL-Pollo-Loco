In this game, you'll take control of Pepe, a character running through the Mexican desert, fighting off attacking chickens by throwing salsa bottles. To win the game, you'll need to defeat an ultimate boss. Use the right and left arrow keys to move, spacebar to jump, and press 'D' to toss the salsa bottles. Be sure to conserve enough bottles for the final boss, as they can only be defeated with these projectiles.
